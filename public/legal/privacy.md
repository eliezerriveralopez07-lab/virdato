# Privacy Policy (Draft)

## What we collect
- account identifiers (wallet, OAuth IDs)
- usage analytics (if enabled)
- integrity metadata (anti-abuse signals)

## How we use it
- operate the service
- prevent abuse / enforce integrity
- produce reward eligibility outputs

## Data security
Encryption at rest and in transit. Access is restricted.

## Your rights
You may request deletion of certain off-chain data, subject to compliance and fraud prevention.
