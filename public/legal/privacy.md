# Privacy Policy (Final Draft v1)

Last updated: 2026-01-XX

## 1. Data We Collect
- Wallet address(es)
- Linked account identifiers (OAuth IDs) when you connect platforms
- Usage analytics (if enabled)
- Integrity metadata to prevent abuse (anti-sybil/anti-fraud signals)
- Claim history (on-chain is public; off-chain metadata is controlled)

## 2. How We Use Data
- operate the service
- provide dashboards and claims status
- prevent fraud and abuse
- compute reward eligibility

## 3. Sharing
We do not sell personal data. We may share:
- service providers (hosting, analytics) under contract
- legal compliance requests where required

## 4. Security
We apply:
- encryption in transit
- least-privilege access
- logging and monitoring

## 5. Retention
We keep off-chain data only as long as needed for operations, compliance, and fraud prevention.

## 6. Your Rights
Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access/deletion of off-chain data.
On-chain data is immutable and cannot be deleted.
