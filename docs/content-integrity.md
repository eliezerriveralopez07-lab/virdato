## AI-Generated Content Policy

Virdato rewards human-generated, integrity-preserving contributions.

### Policy
- AI-generated content is allowed on the platform
- AI-generated content is **not eligible for VIRD rewards**
- Contributors must declare AI usage truthfully

### Enforcement Mechanisms
1. Mandatory AI-origin declaration
2. Automated AI-detection risk scoring
3. Community challenges with bonded staking
4. Slashing for misrepresentation or abuse

### Misrepresentation
Falsely declaring AI-generated content as human-created is considered misconduct and may result in:
- reward forfeiture
- slashing of bonded VIRD
- loss of future reward eligibility

### Governance
All thresholds, enforcement rules, and appeal processes are controlled by DAO governance.
